Teaching Experience

Since my second year of doctoral studies until the completion of my thesis, I taught at INSA Rouen, primarily conducting tutorials (TD) in mathematics. These tutorials covered various topics such as differential equations, arithmetic, and topology. Additionally, I supervised practical sessions (TP) in data science, where I guided students in performing regression and classification tasks using Python on classic datasets.As of October 2024, I joined the IUT of Paris - Rives de Seine as an ATER (temporary teaching and research assistant). I am responsible for the "Math Accueil" course, which is designed to support students with mathematics deficiencies after high school. I also lead tutorials in mathematical analysis. Furthermore, I teach a data mining course, including its practical sessions, where I introduce decision trees (CART) and assist students in practical implementations using R. In the upcoming semester, I will take on three new subjects: probabilities, parametric estimation, and linear regression, combining both tutorials and practical sessions.
